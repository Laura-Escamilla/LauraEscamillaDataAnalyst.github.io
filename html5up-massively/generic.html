Pewlett-Hackard-Analysis

We made an analysis about a large company boasting several thousand employees, among whom is a large number of employees who in the near future will begin to retire. We obtained info like the number of employees to be retired according to their title, the number of positions to be filled, the number of positions to fill grouped by title and department, the number of employees eligible to participate in a Mentorship Program grouped by department and position.
